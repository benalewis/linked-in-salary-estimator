Privacy Policy — LinkedIn Salary Estimator

Effective date: 2026-05-02. This page describes the Chrome extension “LinkedIn Salary Estimator.”

This extension is developed independently and is not affiliated with or endorsed by LinkedIn or Google. If you choose to use optional AI features, you also rely on Google’s services under their terms and privacy policy.

What we do not operate

We do not run our own servers to collect, store, or sell your personal data for this extension. There is no account sign-up in the extension and no analytics SDK controlled by the developer embedded for tracking.

Data stored on your device

The extension may store the following in your browser using chrome.storage.local (local to this profile):

  • Display preferences (for example, chosen currency for labels).
  • If you choose to enter one: a Google Gemini API key and optional model id. This is treated as a secret credential and is kept in local extension storage; it is not intentionally transmitted to servers operated by the extension developer.
  • Cached estimate responses or related small payloads so repeat visits may avoid repeating identical requests where implemented.

Website content processed on LinkedIn pages

On LinkedIn profile pages you visit, the extension reads public page content visible in the DOM as needed to render the injected panel (for example, role titles, employers, dates, summaries). Processing occurs in the context of pages you have opened.

Optional use of Google Gemini (third party)

When you configure a Gemini API key, the extension’s service worker sends HTTPS requests to Google’s Generative Language API (generativelanguage.googleapis.com) with prompts and related context needed to generate text. That may include excerpts derived from the LinkedIn page you are viewing.

Those requests are governed by Google’s policies and your Google account. Review Google’s Privacy Policy and Gemini API terms as applicable.

Chrome Web Store “data usage” alignment

For transparency with the Chrome Web Store data disclosure form, this extension may handle authentication information (your user-supplied API key) and website content (public LinkedIn page text in open tabs) as described above. It does not intentionally collect a browsing history list, health data, payment card data, or personal communications content for developer-side processing.

Children

This extension is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy when the extension changes. The “effective date” at the top will be revised when meaningful updates are made.
